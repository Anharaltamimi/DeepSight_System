About Us

Our Story

This system is part of a graduation project aimed at creating an AI-powered tool to assist ophthalmologists in diagnosing Diabetic Maculopathy through OCT images. It combines deep learning technology with clinical expertise to enhance early detection and improve treatment outcomes.

Our Vision

To revolutionize ophthalmology by providing fast, accurate, and AI-driven diagnostics, enabling better patient care and earlier interventions for Diabetic Maculopathy.

Our Mission

To develop a reliable AI system that empowers clinicians with advanced OCT image analysis, supporting them in making informed decisions and improving treatment efficiency.

Our Project Team

This system was developed as a graduation project by our dedicated team of students under the supervision of Dr. Ghada Emam.
